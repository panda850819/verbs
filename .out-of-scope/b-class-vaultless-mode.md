## What was rejected

B-class target-audience support through a bundled vault-less or start-from-zero
mode.

## Why

Source text: `B-class TA (no vault, want to start from zero). Bundling a vault-less mode adds a second product surface; not worth the complexity in v2.`

The v2 public-ready scope stays focused on users who bring a vault.

## What would reopen it

Revisit only after the 30-day decision freeze if fresh B-class users create
enough repeated demand to justify a second product surface.
